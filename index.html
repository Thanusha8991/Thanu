About Me

Artist!

I am an undergraduate student at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka and an artist currently living in Jaffna, Sri Lanka. I always prefer to do portrait drawings and digital art. My role model of art is Silvie Mahdal. I'm also interested in programming language. I've learned Java, C, C++ and also HTML, CSS and JS. I also worked as Frontend developer with my colleagues in MCS IT CAMPUS.
